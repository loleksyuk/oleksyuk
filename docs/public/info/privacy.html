oleksyuk Privacy

How we handle your data

🔒 TL;DR: We respect your privacy

oleksyuk runs entirely in your browser. We don't collect personal data, track your activity, or use cookies for advertising. Your game progress is stored locally on your device.

📊 Data Collection

oleksyuk is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we do and don't collect:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No tracking cookies or analytics
  • No third-party advertising trackers
  • No account creation required

💾 Local Storage

Some features use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Game progress and high scores
  • Casino balance (virtual currency only)
  • User preferences and settings
  • Color picker saved colors

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser data.

🔗 External Links

oleksyuk contains links to external websites (Discord servers, AI tools, etc.). These sites have their own privacy policies that we don't control. Please review their policies before using them.

🛡️ Security

The tab cloaker and about:blank features are provided for privacy purposes. Users are responsible for how they use these tools. We don't endorse bypassing school or workplace restrictions.
